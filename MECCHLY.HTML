About Us

Mecchilly Technologies (T) Ltd is a private engineering company based in Arusha, Tanzania, established in 2011. We specialize in the design, construction, maintenance, and management of civil, mechanical, and electrical infrastructure. With a strong foundation under the Companies Act No. 12 of 2002, we are committed to delivering quality projects and reliable services.
